# Orquestra – Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence

Orquestra represents coordination, governance, and structured flow of modular systems.
The visual identity reflects leadership (conductor) and controlled operational flow (modular bars).

---

## 2. Logo System

### Primary Logo
Horizontal composition including symbol + logotype.

### Symbol
Standalone conductor + modular flow elements.
Used for:
- App icons
- Social avatars
- Favicon

### Icon Version
Optimized square layout for UI and product usage.

---

## 3. Color Palette

| Role              | Color Name | Hex     |
|-------------------|------------|---------|
| Primary Blue      | Deep Navy  | #1E3A5F |
| Secondary Blue    | Steel Blue | #2C6E91 |
| Accent Teal       | Teal       | #1CA7A6 |

### Usage Rules
- Primary Blue for typography and core elements
- Teal for accent and emphasis
- Avoid excessive gradient usage
- Prefer flat and clean rendering

---

## 4. Typography

Recommended:
- Sans-serif
- Clean and modern
- Examples: Inter, Helvetica, SF Pro, Arial

Weight recommendation:
- Logo: SemiBold / 600
- UI text: Regular / 400

---

## 5. Spacing

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the conductor head around the logo.

---

## 6. Background Usage

Preferred:
- White
- Very light gray

Dark mode:
- Logo can be inverted to white while preserving teal accent

---

## 7. Brand Personality

Orquestra is:
- Structured
- Strategic
- Enterprise-ready
- Minimal
- Data-driven

It is NOT:
- Playful
- Overly colorful
- Decorative
- Startup-chaotic
